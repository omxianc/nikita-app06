Info

This application is used for media viewing and sharing across users social networks. User can make his own gallery by uploading files into it and then viewing them from inside of the application. The aplication version may change in the futur and have newer versions of it avalable.

Picture gallery

Picture gallery is a place where you can view all sorts of beautiful images, pictures, photos and art work! If you see anything you like you can leave a comment and a like or even a dislike something if you want. You can view the image in a larger size by clicking on it and enjoy the scenery. If you have something to say to the creator of the image you can write a comment just below the image you are looking at, leave a like or even a dislike if you wish so.


Videos

Videos are like a beautiful picture that moves and may even speak. By clicking on a video that you would like to watch you consume information about a story that it shows. Much like in the picture gallery where every picture also is telling its own story, the video gallery acts the same way. Leaving a like and writing a comment is much appreciated.




About music

Music is a great way to adjust your emotions and feel the mood you are in at the time. Try pressing the button at the corner of the screen and select the music that you feel is appropriate for your mood right now. Music can also make your mood so don't be shy to of using the music player. And while you are listening to a good song feel free to browse the websites gallery for images that goes along with your mood, sit back, relax and enjoy.

About likes & dislikes

Likes are a good way to show that you enjoy someone's work, and support that person in that matter, as well as encourage them to make even more awesome art work or photos. Usually people misunderstand the importance of a dislike and that is why most sites don't even provide an option for that, because it brings out the negativity. But in reality taking a dislike on one of your posts can make the quality of your next one a lot better. And in the long run the quality of the gallery will also grow with the quality of the pictures.
